our story

Rocky Mtn Coffee House owners made a pit stop in Denver, Colorado, bringing a mastery of coffee roasting knowledge with us. We changed the Denver coffee scene with one concept: fresh roasted coffee, when you want it. Over the years, and through plenty of trial and error, we have a learned a bit about what it takes to make those little green coffee beans sing. We have an awesome shop in the Denver Metro area. It's safe to say we are pretty jazzed about all of this, and hope you will come visit us. There is nothing we love more than talking coffee with our customers.

what we do

Rocky Mt Coffee is a small batch, specialty coffee roaster in Denver, Colorado. We pride ourselves in working with single estate and privately owned farms. We firmly believe that the best way to impact farmers is to encourage them to sustainably produce higher-quality coffee. We are proud to have direct trade connections with 100% of the coffees we bring in to our Roast works for wholesale and retail. This includes coffees from South America, Africa, and others. We specialize in very sweet, highly vibrant, and exceptionally clean coffees with a variety of flavors in the final cup.
